Hazrat El Sheikh Syed Mubarik Ali Shah Gillani

A Legacy of Spiritual Guidance and Community Building

About

Hazrat El Sheikh Syed Mubarik Ali Shah Gillani (RA) was a revered Sufi leader, scholar, and founder of The Muslims of America (TMOA) and the International Qur’anic Open University (IQOU). His life was dedicated to spiritual guidance, education, and community building.
